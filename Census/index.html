I created this application to explore the US Census beyond looking at the attribute table. My goal was to make the US Census as interactive for the user as possible. The Census is not only important for the US government, but also for US residents to discover their neighborhoods.

Using ESRI Smart Mapping you are able to better visualize the data. You can use the interactive legend to adjust the layer render to better explore the data that interests you. You will be able to use an address locator or location button to find the area you are seeking. To find out more about this map please follow this link.

Special thanks to ESRI and the US Census Bureau for making this data available.
