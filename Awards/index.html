ESRI Data Viz App Challenge

In 2015 I recieved 3rd place in the Esri Data Viz App Challenge with my Explore your neighborhood through the US Census application.

"The Esri Data Viz App Challenge asked geodevelopers to build brilliant, interactive, meaningful mapping apps that showcase the data visualization power of ArcGIS. The winners wowed the judges with these apps." ESRI

ESRI Human Health and Climate Change App Challenge

In 2015 I recieved 3rd place in the ESRI Human Health and Climate Change App Challenge with my Sea-Level Rise and Its Impact application.

"Do you have an innovative idea for an app that could help health and human services professionals understand the impacts of climate change on the health of their communities, and drive action to meet the population's needs?" ESRI
